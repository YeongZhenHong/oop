setup(self)

Setup sequence where base cases are generated for comparison

everything.cvs contains punctuations, links, numbers, new lines, symbols, stop words and capital letters

negative and positive are strings that produce negative and postitive sentiments respectively

negative_result and postive_result are the expected return values when the strings are passed into the Analysis func

test_neg and test_pos are negative and postive scores to be passed into plot_pie func

test_neg_img and test_pos_img are the expected .png created when the base cases are executed
